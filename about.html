About me

a trip down memory lane...

From the age of 5, I have been intrigued by games. I started with the Super Nintendo and later on I managed to get a Nintendo 64. I got my first real PC when I was 9, which I build with my uncle.

When I was 11 or 12, I followed a small course for kids on how to build your own website. I can't even remember what I made, but I always remembered a bit of how to do it. Everything has changed in the last 2 decades, but for the better.

The 2 decades after that I have mostly spend my time on PC gaming.

This decade

and now ...

The last 10 years have been a blast. I met my wife, we bought our first home and later upgraded it to a much bigger 'family' home. 2 years ago I became a father and we've been slowly progressing through ranks in the restaurant business but something was missing.
We both weren't truely doing what we wanted to do. I love creating things and development is perfect for that!

Hello World!

And so it began...

Every (web)developer starts with these 2 words on their blank homepage. It felt like a victory, but I had no clue of how much was yet to come. Within 3 months I finished my first project: Foodfreedom.

I have been doing this since July 2021. At the time of writing this, which is early January of 2022, I have converged this website from CSS to SASS and it's been amazing to work with.

I am currently specializing in React.js.
